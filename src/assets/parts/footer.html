Methodology

We used Socialblade.com data to isolate the YouTube channel in every country with the most all-time views and estimate the lifetime earnings for each content creator by applying the midpoint of YouTube's current average APM - payout per 1,000 views - to their view count, excluding musicians and channels owned by media agencies.
